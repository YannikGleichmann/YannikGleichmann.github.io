### Math
- 2023: **Dr. Phil. Mathematik** at the University of Basel, Switzerland, in the research group numerical analysis of Prof. Dr. Marcus Grote.
  The topic of my thesis was on *Adaptive Spectral Inversion for Inverse Medium Problems*, https://edoc.unibas.ch/96027/, where I developed, improved, and proved convergence of an algorithm to solve (nonlinear) inverse problems or optimization problems. To solve the underlying forward problem, consisting of a partial differential equation, I developed my own 2-dimensional finite element library, entirely written in MATLAB. Vectorized and object-oriented! You can find my library here: https://github.com/YannikGleichmann/2dFemLib
